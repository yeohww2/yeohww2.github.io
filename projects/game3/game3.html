🎉 Virtual World Treasure Hunt 🎉

Join the adventure! Help the user and robot collect treasures while avoiding traps. Who can collect all treasures with the least steps? Let's find out! 🎮

📜 Game Rules

  • Click on a cell to move and collect treasures 💎
  • Avoid traps ⚠️ – they will stop you for one round!
  • You and the robot start at the same point, but can you collect all treasures first?
  • Have fun! The one who collects all the treasures with fewer steps wins!

Press "Start Game" to begin!
